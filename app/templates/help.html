Help & FAQ

🔰 Getting Started

  • Create an Account: Go to the Register page and fill in your username, email, and password.
  • Password Requirements: Your password must include:
    • ✔ At least 8 characters
    • ✔ One lowercase letter
    • ✔ One uppercase letter
    • ✔ One number
    • ✔ One special character (!@#$%^&*)
  • Confirm Password: Re-enter the same password to confirm it.

🔐 Login Help

  • Login: Go to the Login page and enter your registered username or email and password.
  • Forgot Password? Click the “Forgot your password?” link on the login page.
  • Invalid Login? Make sure:
    • Your username or email is typed correctly.
    • Your password is correct and case-sensitive.

🔁 Resetting Your Password

  • Start the process: Click “Forgot your password?” on the login page.
  • Enter your email: Use the same email you used while registering.
  • Check your inbox: You will receive a reset link via email. (Check spam if not found.)
  • Reset password: Use the link to go to the reset page and enter your new password.
  • Password must meet the rules:
    • ✔ At least 8 characters
    • ✔ One lowercase letter
    • ✔ One uppercase letter
    • ✔ One number
    • ✔ One special character (!@#$%^&*)
  • Done: You can now log in with your new password.

Note: If your reset link has expired or is invalid, repeat the "Forgot password" process to receive a new one.

🔎 Searching for Events

  • Search All Events: Use the search bar on the homepage to find events locally.
  • Filter by Keyword: Enter keywords such as event names or themes.
  • Filter by City or State: Use the dropdowns or input fields to narrow results by location.
  • Filter by Start Date: Choose a start date to view events beginning on or after the selected date.
  • View Details: Click on an event card to see full details, RSVP, or add it to your favorites.

❤️ Favorites

  • Save events: Click the "Favorite" button on an event page to save it.
  • View favorites: Go to My Favorites from the navigation menu.
  • Remove favorites: Click "Remove" on the Favorites page to unfavorite any event.

📅 RSVPs

  • RSVP to events: Click the "RSVP" button on the event page.
  • View your RSVPs: Visit My RSVPs from the top navigation.
  • Cancel RSVP: Use the cancel option on the RSVP page if your plans change.

👤 Profile

  • Access your profile: Click your username or go to Profile.
  • Update profile picture: Use the "Change Picture" option to upload a new one.
  • Update Email or Password: You can update your email address or password using the provided form fields. Leave a field blank if you don’t wish to change it.
  • Click "Update Profile" to save any changes you've made to your profile.
